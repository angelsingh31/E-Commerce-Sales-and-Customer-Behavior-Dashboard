# 🛒 E-Commerce Sales & Customer Behavior Dashboard (Excel)
## 📊 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Excel dashboard that analyzes e-commerce sales performance and customer behavior. The dashboard helps businesses understand sales trends, purchasing patterns, product popularity, shipping performance, and customer satisfaction.

The goal of this project is to demonstrate data analysis, dashboard design, and business insights generation using Microsoft Excel.

## 🎯 Objectives
Analyze overall sales performance
Identify customer purchasing patterns
Understand product demand by gender
Monitor shipping time efficiency
Evaluate customer satisfaction trends
Provide actionable insights for business decision making

The dataset was cleaned and prepared in Microsoft Excel before creating the dashboard.

## 📈 Dashboard Features
🔑 Key Metrics

The dashboard highlights important business KPIs:
Total Orders: 2,400
Total Quantity Sold: 11,997
Total Revenue: $649K
Average Rating: 4.0
Average Delivery Time: 2.3 days

## 📉 Sales Trend Analysis
A 13-week sales trend chart shows revenue fluctuations over time, helping identify:peak sales weeks,sales growth patterns,potential seasonal demand

## 👥 Purchase Channel Preference
Displays how customers purchase products through different channels:Mobile App,Instagram,Partner App,Target.com,Website
The chart compares purchase behavior across gender segments.

## 🛍️ Top Products by Gender
This visualization identifies the most popular products and highlights differences in male vs female purchasing patterns.
Example insights:
T-Shirts and Jeans dominate total sales
Female customers represent the majority of buyers

## 📦 Number of Items Bought
Shows distribution of how many items customers typically purchase per order.
Insights:
Most customers buy 1–3 items per order,Bulk purchases are less frequent.

## 🚚 Shipping Time Analysis
Tracks delivery time distribution to evaluate logistics performance.
Insights:
Most orders are delivered within 1–3 days,Very few orders exceed 7 days

## ⭐ Customer Satisfaction
Monthly breakdown of customer ratings (1–5).
This helps monitor customer experience trends over time.

## 🌍 Customer Location
A geographic map shows order quantity by region, helping identify:
high demand locations,regional sales performance

## ⚙️ Tools Used
Microsoft Excel

## 💡 Key Insights

Female customers account for over 50% of purchases.
Mobile apps and websites are the most popular purchase channels.
T-Shirts and Jeans are the top selling products.
Most customers purchase 2–3 items per order.
Delivery performance is strong with an average delivery time of ~2 days.
Customer satisfaction remains high with ratings mostly between 4 and 5.
